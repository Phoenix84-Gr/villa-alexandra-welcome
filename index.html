🏛️ Towns Nearby

Kavala 35 min

A hidden Aegean gem blending ancient history with modern charm — cobblestone old town, Byzantine walls, the imposing Kavala Castle, and seaside tavernas with fresh seafood.

📍 Get Directions

Nea Peramos 14 min

Stunning beaches and unique tavernas. Stroll the seaside pedestrian street, relax in the cafés and bars, and enjoy traditional Greek food.

📍 Get Directions

Eleftheroupoli 20 min

One of the oldest towns in Eastern Macedonia, built at the foot of mounts Paggaio and Symbolo. Friday traditional market — fresh fruit and vegetables directly from producers. Visit the historic Holy Church of Agios Nikolaos.

⛱️ Organized Beaches

Ammolofi 12 min

Ranked among the ten most beautiful beaches in Greece. Turquoise waters, long golden sand, beach bars, sunbeds and parking.

📍 Directions

Almyra Beach Bar 6 min

One of the most famous beach bars in the area. Fully organized with beach volleyball, beach tennis and racquetball. Known for parties with well-known DJs and unforgettable nights by the sea.

📍 Directions

Beach Bar Tripa 4 min

A magnificent landscape with views of Apollonia Tower. Coffees and cocktails on comfortable sunbeds and large poufs — crystal-clear sea, mixed Greek and international music.

📍 Directions

Paloma Beach Bar 4 min

In Sarakina — organized beaches that combine simplicity with natural beauty. Small tavernas and canteens with delicious food and drinks nearby.

📍 Directions

🏖️ Quiet Beaches

Paralia Folia 2 min

A rough diamond minutes from the villa — over-aged olives, blooming bougainvillea and the dynamic sea. Perfect for a slow walk and watching the sunrise.

📍 Directions

First Dune Beach (Ammolofi) 12 min

The unorganized side of Ammolofi — turquoise water, golden sand dunes. Bring everything you need (umbrella, towels, food).

📍 Directions

Vrasidas Beach 24 min

Hidden gem between the dunes and Nea Peramos — deep blue water, sandy beach, lots of privacy. Bring snorkel & fins, lots of fish to see. One small taverna for shade at noon.

📍 Directions

Nudist Beach Kavalas 7 min

Quiet wild rocky landscape with a calm sea. No canteens or bins — come organized. For couples or groups who enjoy nudism.

📍 Directions

🌊 Experiences

Loutra Eleftheron Hot Springs 7 min

Natural thermal hot springs cherished since Ancient Greek and Roman times. Not organized — abandoned buildings frame the route. The first hot spring is at the end of the road above the last building; the main natural thermal pool is upstream.

📍 Directions

Tower of Apollonia 3 min

A 14th-century late-Byzantine tower perched on a rugged cliff above the Aegean. Panoramic views of secluded coves, golden beaches and sun-kissed rocks. The sunset is unforgettable.

📍 Directions

Mesoropi Gournes (Waterfalls) 25 min drive + 7 min walk

Drive through the picturesque alleys of Mesoropi to the parking point, then follow the enchanting path to a cold spring and a small waterfall. Explore upstream, cool off, then stop by the award-winning Bostani taverna.

📍 Parking 💧 Waterfall 🍴 Bostani Taverna

Ktima Biblia Chora Winery 28 min

Tour the estate's vineyard, production, ageing and underground cellars, ending with a tasting of the Estate's wines. Reservation required — Monday to Friday, 10:00–14:00.
